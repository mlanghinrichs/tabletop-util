Encounter Utility

Intructions

Set dice and mod numbers for the first type of monster your characters are fighting, then hit 'Add' at the bottom to generate a stat table (and repeat for any other monsters in the combat.) # is the number of monsters of that type you want to generate. For dice-based values, each icon adds 1 die of that type, the -/+ changes the modifier, and the X clears the current setup. Players can be added after monster stats are in place. Good luck!
