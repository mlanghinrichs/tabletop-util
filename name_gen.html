Name Generator

Instructions

If you choose a preset, you don't need to do anything else - just hit 'generate names' and you'll get what you asked for.

To make your own names, add to the template by selecting word elements and hit 'generate' to see the results. Beginning consonant clusters are those like "sm" and "wh" that start words in English, but can never end them; mid/fin consonant clusters are the opposite, such as "rd" and "ld". Try different combinations to see what works.

Custom inputs take the format 'syllable1, syllable2, syllable3' (without the quotes), and it'll randomize between whatever you put in when you hit 'add.' Have fun!
